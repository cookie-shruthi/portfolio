Boost Robotics

Boost Robotics is a nonprofit organization dedicated to making competitive VEX robotics accessible to more students and educators in STEM. By providing a structured, high-impact pathway into the world of engineering, the program transforms beginners into confident competitors ready to tackle the challenges of the modern tech landscape.

Learn more at boostrobotics.org.

My Boost Robotics Journey

During the Over Under 2023-2024 season, I joined Boost Robotics as a student eager to learn. The experience gave me hands-on opportunities to design, build, and program VEX robots while working alongside my team to solve real competition challenges.

For the 2024-2025 season, I served as a tech volunteer. I supported student teams, helped maintain equipment, and shared technical knowledge so more learners could participate in robotics with confidence.

Beginning in 2025, I stepped into the role of an instructor. I now teach new teams the fundamentals of VEX robotics, coach students through engineering and programming best practices, and help grow Boost Robotics as an accessible STEM pathway for our community.
