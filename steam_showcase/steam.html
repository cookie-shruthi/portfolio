About STEAM Showcase

STEAM Showcase brings together students, educators, and families to explore the intersection of science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics. Participants experience demonstrations, learn from student presentations, and engage in creative problem-solving activities.

This showcase celebrates student innovation and provides opportunities for learners to demonstrate their skills through interactive demos, project presentations, and hands-on challenges, collaborate with peers, and inspire the next generation of STEM enthusiasts.
